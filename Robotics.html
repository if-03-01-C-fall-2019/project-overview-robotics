Robotics

About robotics

Robotics consist out of engineering and science that includes mechanical engineering,
electronic engineering, information engineering, computer science, and others.

The people that are developing them deal with the design, construction, operation,
and use of robots, as well as computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and
information processing.

Use cases for Robots

They are often used in dangerous environments(for example bomb detection and deactivation)

Another use case is where humans cannot survive(for example in space, underwater, in high heat or cleaning hazardous materials.

Interesting Facts

The word "robot" comes from a Czech word "robota", which means "drudgery"

Elektro was the first humanoid robot. It was built 1939 by Westinghouse. It was seven feet tall and had a vocabulary of 700 words

A robot killed a factory worker in 1981. A robotic factory arm crushed an employee at the Kawasaki plant

The smallest robot is called a nanobot. It is less than one-thousandth of a milimeter.

Robotics is also often used science,technology,engineering and amthemtaics as a teaching aid

Meaning of Robots

The word "Robot" is used to mean any man-made machine
that can perform work or other actions normally performed
by humans, either automatically or by remote control.
